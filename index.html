About me!

Hello! I'm Eva, and I'm a 19-year-old graphic designer based in New York City. I'm a rising junior at Parsons School of Design.

When I'm not working on projects for school, I'm sketching in my notebooks or catching up on films and shows I need to watch.

I'm very interested in designing posters and involving my work with other forms of artistic media such as film or music. However, I'm open to working on anything and adapting to whatever is needed!
